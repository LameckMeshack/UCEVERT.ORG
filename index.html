who are we?

UCEVANT is a group of brilliant young men and women who are passionate about helping humanity in every way possible.UCEVANT is a newly registered and enthusiatic community-based organization (CBO)

Mission

A community based organization Shining light of all possibilities in Ugenya sub county through empowerment of all groups and economic development.

Vision

To be the best CBO in the country any youth and vulnerable individual can relate within the next 10years..

Philosophy

Each of us should use whatever gift that we have, to serve others, as faithful servants to humanity.
